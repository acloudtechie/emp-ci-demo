Email Addresses

Whenever the Email Utility expects something to be an Email Address there are a number of options for what can be passed in. They are as follows:
  • null
  • com.entellitrak.mail.InternetAddress
  • javax.mail.internet.InternetAddress
  • java.lang.String with more than one address separated by "," or ";"
  • com.entellitrak.user.User
  • net.entellitrak.aea.eu.IEmailAddresses
  • java.util.Collection of Email Addresses (including a Collection)
